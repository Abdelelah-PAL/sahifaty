Privacy Policy for the "Sahifati" App and Website

Last Updated: February 7, 2026

Welcome to "Sahifati." We place the highest importance on your data privacy and the security of your personal information. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide when using our website and mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

A. Account and Profile Information

When registering with "Sahifati," we collect the following information to personalize your experience and improve the recommendation engine:

  • Credentials: Username, encrypted password, or login data via (Google/iPhone/Facebook).
  • Personal Information: Name, gender, year of birth.
  • Geographic Information: Country and state/province.
  • Educational Information: Education level and field of specialization.

B. Usage and Evaluation Data

  • Memorization Record: Verses you have evaluated (mastered, for review, etc.).
  • Content Interests: Topics you interact with to enhance the recommendation engine.

C. Licenses and Transaction Data

  • Purchases: License purchase history (without directly storing credit card details, as payments are processed through trusted payment providers).
  • Activation Codes: Record of generated promo codes or those received through the "Gift" system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the data we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Activation: Verifying license validity and account status.
  • Recommendation Engine: Providing personalized memorization recommendations based on your education level, specialization, and verse mastery rate.
  • Content Management: Enabling the three-tier review system (Editor, Supervisor, Approver) to ensure accurate indexing of Qurâ€™anic topics.
  • Gift System: Managing the distribution of free and owned usage licenses.
  • Communication: Sending notifications about the status of your submissions or important app updates.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties.

  • Public Data: Some information (such as username and index contributions) may be visible to supervisors and approvers as part of the review workflow.
  • Institutions: If you are affiliated with a center or organization using "Institutional Services," the center administrator may be able to view your memorization progress reports.

4. Data Security

We are committed to protecting your data by:

  • Encrypting passwords using advanced algorithms (such as PBKDF2).
  • Using secure communication protocols (HTTPS) for data transmission.
  • Performing regular backups to ensure your memorization and evaluation records are not lost.

5. User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and edit your profile data at any time.
  • Request permanent deletion of your account.
  • Opt out of receiving marketing messages.

6. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in the app or applicable laws. You will be notified of any material changes via email or through an in-app notification.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the technical support email within the app: info@sahifati.com
